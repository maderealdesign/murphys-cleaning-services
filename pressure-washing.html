Pressure Washing FAQs

Will pressure washing damage my surface?

The right pressure and method are chosen for the surface. Older or delicate areas are handled carefully.

Do you clean block paving?

Yes. Block paving, paths and patios are common jobs. Re-sanding can be discussed if needed.

Can you clean commercial entrances?

Yes. Small business entrances, yards and customer-facing areas can be cleaned.
